Quotations are Oblea's core deliverable: the priced document a sales person reviews and sends to a customer. Most are born from an inbound email (see the [quotation lifecycle](/concepts/quotation-lifecycle)) — the webhook creates the row, the agent fills it, and a human approves it. Some are created by hand.

## Statuses

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="PROCESSING" icon="spinner">
    Created when a candidate email arrives. The agent is still filling in the client, line items, and pricing.
  </Card>
  <Card title="READY TO REVIEW" icon="clipboard-check">
    The agent has finished. The quotation is waiting on a human to review and approve it.
  </Card>
  <Card title="SENT" icon="paper-plane">
    Approved and sent to the customer.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>Hand-created quotations default to `draft` instead — the email pipeline creates rows as `PROCESSING`.</Note>

## Related tables

A quotation ties together several tables in the [data model](/concepts/data-model): `quotation` (the editable header), `quotation_items` (the line rows), `suggested_items` (runner-up catalog matches), and `client` (the customer requesting the quote).
